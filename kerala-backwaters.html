More About Kerala Backwaters

The backwaters of Kerala are a unique and diverse ecosystem, home to a variety of flora and fauna. The calm waters provide a perfect habitat for numerous bird species, including **kingfishers, herons, and egrets**. Mangrove forests and water lilies thrive along the shores, adding to the natural beauty.

One of the most iconic experiences in the Kerala backwaters is a **houseboat stay**. Traditionally known as 'kettuvallams,' these wooden boats were once used for transporting rice and spices but have been transformed into luxurious floating accommodations. Houseboats come equipped with modern amenities, delicious local cuisine, and stunning views of the water and surrounding landscapes.
