Sundarbans - The Largest Mangrove Forest

About Sundarbans

The Sundarbans is the **largest mangrove forest in the world**, spanning across **West Bengal, India, and Bangladesh**. Recognized as a **UNESCO World Heritage Site**, it is home to the legendary **Royal Bengal Tiger**.

Formed by the **Ganges, Brahmaputra, and Meghna rivers**, this vast delta is a **unique ecological wonder**, consisting of **dense mangroves, tidal waterways, and diverse wildlife**. The Sundarbans serve as a crucial buffer against coastal erosion and act as a natural **barrier against cyclones and tidal waves**.

Visitors can explore the **Sundarbans National Park, wildlife safaris, thrilling boat cruises, exotic birdwatching tours, and serene watchtowers**, providing an unforgettable wilderness experience.
