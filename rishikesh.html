Rishikesh, nestled in the **foothills of the Himalayas**, is a sacred town known for its **spiritual essence, breathtaking landscapes, and thrilling adventure sports**. Often referred to as the **"Gateway to the Garhwal Himalayas"**, it is one of the holiest cities in India.

Situated along the **majestic Ganges River**, Rishikesh attracts **pilgrims, yogis, and adventure seekers** from around the world. The town is famous for **age-old temples, revered ashrams, and its iconic suspension bridges, Lakshman Jhula and Ram Jhula**.

Beyond its spiritual significance, Rishikesh is also an **adventure hotspot**, offering activities like **river rafting, bungee jumping, trekking, and camping by the Ganges**.
