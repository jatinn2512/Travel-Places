Statue of Unity - The Tallest Statue in the World

About Statue of Unity

The Statue of Unity, located in **Kevadia, Gujarat**, is the **tallest statue in the world**, towering at **182 meters (597 feet)**. It was built in honor of **Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel**, India's first Deputy Prime Minister and the man responsible for the **unification of India‚Äôs princely states** after independence.

Inaugurated on **31st October 2018**, the statue stands as a **symbol of strength, unity, and leadership**. It is surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, including the **Narmada River, the Sardar Sarovar Dam, and lush green valleys**.

Visitors can experience **a high-speed elevator ride to the Viewing Gallery (135m height)**, explore the **Museum & Exhibition Hall**, stroll through the **Valley of Flowers**, and witness the **spectacular laser light and sound show** that narrates the legacy of Sardar Patel.
